Forward Deployed AI Engineer
Location: Remote (US) / Hybrid options available

About the role:
We're looking for a Forward Deployed AI Engineer to sit directly with our enterprise customers,
understand their business problems, and build production AI solutions that solve them. You'll
move fast, work across the stack, and own delivery from prototype to production.

What you'll do:
- Partner directly with customer stakeholders to translate business problems into technical
  requirements and working software.
- Design and build Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines over customer data sources.
- Build and deploy LLM-powered applications using APIs from OpenAI, Anthropic (Claude), or
  Google (Gemini).
- Build agents using frameworks such as LangChain, LangGraph, or CrewAI that can use tools and
  make multi-step decisions.
- Write clean, well-documented, production-quality Python code with tests.
- Deploy applications on a cloud platform (AWS, Azure, or GCP).
- Build evaluation harnesses to measure and improve system accuracy over time.
- Travel up to 50% to customer sites as needed.

What we're looking for:
- 1+ years of hands-on experience with LLM APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, or Gemini).
- Experience building RAG pipelines and working with vector databases.
- Experience with at least one agent framework (LangChain, LangGraph, or CrewAI).
- Strong Python fundamentals; SQL experience a plus.
- Experience with at least one major cloud platform (AWS, Azure, or GCP).
- Excellent communication skills -- comfortable presenting technical work to non-technical
  stakeholders.
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field, or equivalent practical experience.

Nice to have:
- Experience with Kubernetes or container orchestration.
- Experience with multi-agent systems.
- Prior client-facing or consulting experience.
